About Me

I am a Ph.D. student working with Dr. Jaichander Swaminathan at IISc Bengaluru. My research focuses on thermodynamic optimal design of a vapor compression desalination system. I have previously worked on the impact of climate change on energy demand, fuel cell systems, and LED-based solar simulator design.
