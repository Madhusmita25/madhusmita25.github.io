About Me

I am a Ph.D. student working with Dr. Jaichander Swaminathan at the Energy Research Centre, IISc Bengaluru. My research focuses on thermodynamic optimization of vapor compression desalination systems. Previously, I worked on LED-based solar simulators, turbojet–turbofan comparative studies, and fuel cell system modeling.
