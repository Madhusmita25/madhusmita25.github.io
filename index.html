About

I am a Ph.D. student working with Dr. Jaichander Swaminathan at the Energy Research Centre, IISc Bengaluru. My work focuses on the thermodynamic optimal design of vapor compression desalination systems. Previously, I worked on understanding the impact of climate change on heating/cooling demand, optimization of fuel-cell systems, and LED-based solar simulator design.
