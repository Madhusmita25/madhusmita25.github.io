About

I am a Ph.D. student working with Dr. Jaichander Swaminathan at the Energy Research Centre, IISc Bengaluru. My research focuses on the thermodynamic optimal design of vapor compression desalination systems. Previously, I have worked on the impact of climate change on heating/cooling demand, optimization of fuel cell systems, and LED-based solar simulator design.

Research Interests

Fuel Cell Systems
Vapor Compression Systems
Thermal Systems Design
Batch Operation and Process Optimization
