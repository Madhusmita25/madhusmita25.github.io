About Me

I am a PhD Student working at the Water Energetics Lab at Energy Research in IISc. During PhD, my work focuses on thermodynamic optimal design of a vapor compression desalination system. I have previously worked on understanding the impact of climate change on heating/cooling, energy demand, fuel cell systems, and solar simulator design.
