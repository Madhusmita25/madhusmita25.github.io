About Me

I am a Ph.D. student working with Dr. Jaichander Swaminathan at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru. During PhD, my work focuses on thermodynamic optimal design of a vapor compression desalination system. I have previously worked on understanding the impact of climate change on heating/cooling, energy demand, fuel cell systems, and solar simulator design .
