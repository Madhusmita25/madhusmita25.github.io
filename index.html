About Me

I am a Ph.D. student working under Dr. Jaichander Swaminathan at the Energy Research Centre, IISc Bengaluru. My research focuses on thermodynamic optimal design of vapor compression desalination systems. My prior work includes LED-based solar simulator design, turbojet–turbofan comparative analysis, and optimization of fuel cell systems.
